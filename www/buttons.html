INDOOR AQ INDEX

Categories

Tap the button depends on Air Quality condition.

Excellent
Tips:
- Air quality is in perfect condition, no need for precautions
Fine
Tips:
- The air quality is still tolerable, you may continue your work.
Moderate
Tips:
- Buy indoor plants to freshen the air.
- Open airways such as windows and doors for better ventilation if the weather is good.
- Keep the area properly ventilated
- Keep it clean.
Poor
Tips:
- Improved the area's ventilation.
- Use a natural ways opening the windows and the door if the weather is permits or good.
- Maintain the cleanliness of the area.
Very Poor
Tips:
- Check for possible Oxygen and Acetylene gas leaks.
- Open airways such as windows and doors for better ventilation if the weather is good.
- Use the ventilation.
- Stop the Fabrication atleast 10-15 mins.
- Maintain the cleanliness of the area.
- Wear PPE.
Severe
Tips:
- Temporary stop the fabrication.
- Wear a mask and safety goggles.
- Wear PPE.
- Sanitize the area and leave the area
